Our Story

Welcome to Pillow Haven, your number one source for all things pillows. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of comfort, with a focus on quality, customer service, and uniqueness.

Founded in 2020 by Mark Andrie Atienza, Pillow Haven has come a long way from its beginnings in a small home office. When Mark first started out, his passion for eco-friendly and comfortable pillows drove his to quit her day job, and gave her the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into a booming online store. We now serve customers all over the world, and are thrilled to be a part of the quirky, eco-friendly wing of the e-commerce industry.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Our History

2020 - Founded

Pillow Haven was founded, starting in a small home office with the goal of creating eco-friendly sleeping solutions.

2021 - Eco-friendly Line

We launched our first eco-friendly pillow line, utilizing sustainable materials to benefit both sleep quality and the environment.

2022 - Custom Designs

Our product range expanded to include custom designs, allowing customers to personalize their sleep experience.

2023 - Global Reach

We reached 10,000 happy customers worldwide, marking a significant milestone in our global expansion.
